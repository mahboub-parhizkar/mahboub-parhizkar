#### 🔬 Active Research

**[ContextFlow](https://github.com/orionai-research/contextflow)** — *OrionAI Research Lab*

> Runtime-Adaptive Orchestration for LLM-Native Multi-Agent Systems in Operational Environments

Investigating whether multi-agent AI systems can reason about their own coordination strategy at runtime — selecting and adapting how agents work together based on observed operational context signals, rather than following a strategy hardcoded at design time.

Core research question:
> *How can LLM-native multi-agent systems dynamically adapt their orchestration strategies at runtime based on operational context signals — and what are the measurable effects on coordination quality, efficiency, and reliability?*
